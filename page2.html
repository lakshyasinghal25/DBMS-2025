Dental Implants Inventory DBMS

Project Description

This project is a Database Management System (DBMS) specifically designed for dental implant businesses. It provides an organized and efficient way to store, retrieve, and manage data related to dental implants, tools, and other medical materials used in clinics and dental laboratories.

The system maintains detailed records of all inventory items, including product names, categories, quantities, suppliers, and expiry dates. It supports essential DBMS operations such as data insertion, updating, deletion, and querying, making it easy to track stock levels and manage material flow.

Our DBMS solution helps dental businesses avoid stockouts and overstocking by providing real-time inventory visibility. It also improves data accuracy, ensures consistency, and enhances decision-making by providing a reliable and centralized database system tailored to the needs of dental clinics.
